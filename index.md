This site is an automated weekly research briefing: every Friday morning a
[GitHub Actions pipeline](https://github.com/ashwin-ned/ash-research-briefing)
fetches new papers from arXiv and Hugging Face Daily Papers, filters and
ranks them against an editable set of research interests, and publishes the
selected papers below with abstracts and relevance-framed summaries. Subscribe
via [RSS](/feed.xml) to get the briefing without checking back manually.
